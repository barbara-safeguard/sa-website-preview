About

I am the brand, and safeguarding is personal to me

Barbara Veeramallay-Permaul Founder, Independent Safeguarding Consultant & DSL My Leadership  |  The Quiet Difference

I'm Barbara Veeramallay-Permaul, founder of Safeguarding Assured by BVPSkills, an independent safeguarding consultant and Designated Safeguarding Lead. I bring more than twenty years protecting people across education, faith communities and international mission settings, and the last two years helping religious congregations turn safeguarding from an audit worry into lived, evidenced assurance.

As a DSL, I have owned policy, compliance and case management across multi-site organisations, built frameworks aligned with Ofsted and CSSA expectations, and delivered training in child protection, PREVENT, online safety and trauma-informed practice, working in close partnership with local authorities, social care and multi-agency safeguarding hubs.

My practice is grounded in integrity, compassion and accountability, creating environments where children, young people, vulnerable adults and staff are protected, listened to and empowered. I serve as a DSL for several religious charities and as a safeguarding ambassador within the Catholic Church, bringing both board-level judgement and hands-on practitioner experience to every engagement.

Services

Focused support, built around your setting

Every engagement is tailored to your context, culture, people and responsibilities.

Audit Sprint Sessions

Intensive support before or after an audit, to review evidence, identify gaps, prioritise risk and build a clear action plan that leaders and trustees can actually use.

Audit & Inspection Readiness

CSSA and Ofsted preparation with clear evidence, data-led dashboards and audit-ready tools, so you can demonstrate compliance with confidence.

Policy & Document Development

Bespoke policies, trackers, trustee reports and process flows, structured around the relevant framework, the CSSA Standards or Ofsted and KCSIE, and your document control framework.

Safeguarding Awareness Training

Bespoke awareness sessions, tailored to each organisation, that are practical, values-led and relevant to your people and responsibilities.

Assurance & Monthly Reporting

Assurance frameworks and monthly safeguarding reporting that give leaders and boards a clear, current picture of safeguarding maturity and risk.

Governance & NED Support

Independent scrutiny for trustee boards and governing bodies, strengthening safeguarding oversight and governance arrangements.
