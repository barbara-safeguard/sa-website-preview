21 June 2026 Welcome

Welcome to Safeguarding Assured

Thank you for visiting. I started Safeguarding Assured by BVPSkills to help religious congregations and education settings move from good intention to clear, confident, auditable practice, and to make safeguarding feel like something that is lived day to day, not something that sits in a folder.

This is where I will share regular updates: reflections on practice, plain-language summaries of changes to the CSSA National Safeguarding Standards, lessons from audit preparation, and practical ideas you can use in your own setting. My aim is for each piece to leave you a little more prepared, supported and assured.

If there is a safeguarding topic you would like me to cover, I would be glad to hear from you.

Barbara Veeramallay-Permaul, Founder
